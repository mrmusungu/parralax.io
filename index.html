About Us

Nemm Tours and Safaris is a tour company locally owned and operated by experienced Kenyans.The Directors, Nick and Paul have the best safari stories to tell, having been driver guides for over fifteen years. All our drivers and guides speak fluent English. German, French and Italian drivers and guides are available on request. We do not sit in the office but love the adventure out there in the field as your driver guides. Hence, all your expectations will be well taken care of. Our tailor-made Itineraries are well planned to make sure you get the best value of what you pay for. Book your next photography Safari adventure with us. Our team understands the technical aspects of taking good wildlife and scenery photos. Get your group together and we’ll tailor-make a photography safari just for you.
